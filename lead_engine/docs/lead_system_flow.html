Lead Intelligence System

US Personal-Injury Law Firms  ·  5–50 attorneys  ·  built on ScrapeGraphAI + gpt-4o-mini  ·  runs locally, outputs a CSV
In one line: find PI law firms → fetch & qualify each site → identify the decision-maker and their email → verify it → score & rank → a clean CSV of outreach-ready leads. No agency, no Apify/Apollo seat, ~$10–50/month.

The Pipeline — 6 stages

1
DISCOVER free
Google Places API (firm name, website, phone, rating) or Apollo + ExportApollo (name, email, title, website — ~$3.49/1k). Produces a raw list of candidate firms.
▼
2
ENRICH free
ScrapeGraphAI + Playwright fetch each firm's site. Plain httpx first; a real headless browser falls back automatically for Cloudflare-blocked and JavaScript-rendered sites.
▼
3
QUALIFY ~$10/mo
gpt-4o-mini reads the homepage + team page: is it a law firm? practice areas? attorney count (the real 5–50 gate) and the decision-maker's name. Plus free regex signals: FAQ, schema, Google-Ads pixel.
▼
4
FIND EMAIL free first
Published email on the site (incl. Cloudflare-decoded) → else pattern-guess from the decision-maker's name (jsmith@, john.smith@) → optional Hunter/Apollo fallback for misses.
▼
5
VERIFY free
Syntax → MX → SMTP probe. Confirms the mailbox is real and flags catch-all domains. Send confidently to valid; pay a few cents (MillionVerifier) only on the ambiguous bucket.
▼
6
SCORE & EXPORT free
Weighted score from the signals → Tier A / B / C. Permanent dedup by domain + email. Appends to leads.csv — firm, decision-maker, verified email, phone, score, tier, practice-area type.

Three Ways to Run It (pick by budget)

Lean & free

~$10/mo
  • Google Places + free finder
  • Free SMTP verification
  • Best $0-extra coverage
  • Lower email hit-rate on catch-all firms

Apollo source cheapest leads

~$12–15/mo
  • ExportApollo ~$3.49/1k leads
  • Free SMTP verification
  • Name+email+website in one CSV
  • Unverified; coverage on tiny firms varies; manual batch export

Hunter source highest quality

~$45–60/mo
  • Hunter Starter (2,000 finds)
  • Auto-verified on arrival
  • API, fully automatable
  • Best for catch-all / Google-hosted firms

Sourcing: Google Places vs ExportApollo

Google PlacesExportApollo
GivesFirm + website + phoneFirm + website + decision-maker + email

Recommended start: run a $2 / 500-lead ExportApollo test through the pipeline and measure two numbers — % that are genuine 5–50 attorney PI firms, and % of emails that pass verification. If both look good, Apollo becomes the cheap primary source; if coverage on small firms is thin, keep Google Places primary and use Apollo to fill gaps.
